Welcome to Our Sponsorship Program!

We invite businesses and organizations to be part of our vibrant festival by becoming a sponsor. This annual event is a celebration of community, culture, and entertainment, attracting thousands of attendees from across the region. As a sponsor, you'll have the unique opportunity to increase your brand visibility and connect with the community in a meaningful way.

Sponsorship Levels

We offer various levels of sponsorship to suit your organization's needs and goals:

Platinum Sponsor

  1. Benefits: Prime logo placement on all festival materials, main stage naming rights, largest booth space, VIP access to events, and special recognition in press releases and social media.
  2. Investment: $3000

Gold Sponsor

  1. Benefits: Prominent logo placement, secondary stage naming rights, large booth space, recognition in select press materials, and social media highlights.
  2. Investment: $1000

Silver Sponsor

  1. Benefits: Logo placement on festival website and select materials, medium booth space, and social media mentions.
  2. Investment: $750

Bronze Sponsor

  1. Benefits: Name listing on the festival website, small booth space, and social media acknowledgment.
  2. Investment: $500

Community Partner

  1. Benefits: Suitable for small businesses and local organizations, this level includes name listing on select materials and website.
  2. Investment: $250

Custom Sponsorship Opportunities

We understand that each sponsor has unique goals. If you have specific ideas or requests, we are happy to work with you to create a custom sponsorship package.

Why Sponsor Our Festival?

  1. Brand Exposure: Reach a large and diverse audience through festival branding, advertising, and social media.
  2. Community Engagement: Show your commitment to the community by supporting a beloved local event.
  3. Networking Opportunities: Connect with other businesses, local leaders, and festival attendees.
  4. Media Coverage: Gain exposure through media coverage of the festival.

Become a Sponsor Today!
